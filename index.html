About AgriLocal

AgriLocal is a mobile marketplace that bridges the gap between local agricultural vendors and conscious consumers. We believe in supporting sustainable local food systems and empowering farmers to reach customers directly.

Built with cutting-edge technology including React Native, Expo, and Supabase, AgriLocal offers a seamless experience for both buyers and vendors, promoting healthier communities and sustainable agriculture.
